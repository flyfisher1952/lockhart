Note for:   Michael Lee LOCKHART,   23 Sep 1952 -          Index

Individual Note:
     MISSION: I served in the Bogota, Colombia, SA mission from 2 Nov 1978 to 22
Nov 1980.

MISSION: I recieved my call 10 Sep 1978 (?) and received my endowment 19 Sep
1978 in the Salt Lake Temple. I spent 4 (LONG) months in the MTC before going
to the Arcadia, California Mission (21 Feb 1979) to wait for a visa. I
finally left for Bogota (21 Mar 1979) and arrived 22 Mar 1979. My mission
presidents were President Olsen and President Dunn (last 6 months).

MISSION: I learned the following lessons:

MISSION: 1) Love the Lord and have faith in Him
2) Love your companion
3) Love the people where you serve
4) Trust in and seek the guidance of the Spirit in all things
5) The Lord does the work, we only act as imperfect messengers.

MISSION: (All of the above presuppose: Obedience to the commandments,
especially the mission rules, daily prayer and scripture study)
