Note for:   Iantha Rose BLAKE,   7 Oct 1877 - 8 Nov 1950         Index

Individual Note:
     First Robert Blake of Wiltshire, England and brother Nicholas were
French Hugenots driven out of France in the later part of 13th Century.
Their names originally Cadiux or Cadew. After coming to England it was
changed to Le Blaque or The Black, Afterwards called Blake.
