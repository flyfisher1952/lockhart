Note for:   Anastasia Lee LOCKHART,   7 Jul 1996 -          Index

Individual Note:
     Anastasia was adopted by us in Kerch,Crimea,Ukraine Dec 24, 2001. Her birth name was Anastasia Sergeyevna Ocipova. She was born to Sergey (a Russian) and Anna (Ukrainian) Ocipova.

Sergey was a fisherman. Anna died from burns (5th degree) she got from boiling water over 40% of her body. We don't know how it happened at this time. Sergey apparently drank himself to death and died from related heart problems. Anastasia told us that he drank a lot of "stinky water" and a doctor there told us that the heart related problem listed on the death certificate (stenosis coronary sclerosis) was commonly caused by alcohol and listed as the cause of deth in such cases. It was standard procedure in Ukraine to list that condition as the cause of death when someone overdosed on alcohol.

Anna died in the October 21, 1999 and Sergey in March 7 2000.

Individual Notes
Note for:   Sergey Ocipova,    - Mar 2000         Index

Individual Note:
     Sergey was the natural father of Anastasia, adopted daughter of Micheal and Tracie Lockhart. He died of an alcohol overdose in March of 1999, six months after the death of his wife Anna. She died of burns to 45% of her body caused by boiling water. We don't know how this happened, but some speculation is allowed. Most buildings in Ukraine are heated by boiler systems. I think that she either worked with such a system or was working on one and suffered an accident.
