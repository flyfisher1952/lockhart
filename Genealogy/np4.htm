Note for:   Zachary Cole LOCKHART,   15 Feb 1995 -          Index

Individual Note:
     Adoption: Zachary Cole was adopted through LDS Social Services by us (Mike &
Tracie) on 16 Feb 1995. His birth mother and birth grandmother talked to us
that evening for about an hour. We found them very pleasant to talk to.
Although we weren't supposed to find out each others names, we both slipped
and used them. So I suppose they know ours as we do theirs (Jean and Lorretta
Searsy). Jean was 5'11" with a slender/medium build, blonde hair and
grey-brown eyes. She looked a lot like Tracie, enough to be sisters. Her
mother was smaller and had dark hair. Both were intelligent, attractive, well
spoken, and had good senses of humor. Lorretta had the hardest time giving
Zachary up. Jean really didn't know the magnitude of her gift to us. She
knew it in her mind, but Lorretta knew it in her heart and was struggling.
They remarked how much I resemble Jean's father (or was it her grandfather?).

Adoption: Zachary was the most beautiful baby we'd ever seen, or at least one
of the two most beautiful, Mathieu being the other most beautiful. We would
have picked Zachary up the night before, but he was having a little trouble
eating so they wanted to keep him overnight.
