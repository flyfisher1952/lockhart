Note for:   Adam Rankin,   1688 - 4 May 1747         Index

Individual Note:
     BIOGRAPHY: His wife MAry Steele was of the landed gentry of Scotland. He came
to America in 1720, landing in Philadelphia and settling in Chester co., PA.
There he had 200 acres of land, Nov. 16, 1737. He had 100 acres of land in
Lancaster Co, Nov. 11, 1742. His will is filed in Lancaster Co. and in
Cumberland Co.

Note for:   John Moseley,   1639 - 8 Oct 1661         Index

Individual Note:
     BIOGRAPHY: His name was changed from Maudesley to Moseley and came from
England on the "Mary and John" in 1630. He settled in Dochester, Mass. in 1631.
    He was burried in Dorchester a few feet north of Rev William Mather. His
signature is on the document establishing the first free school.

Note for:   Thomas Hartshorn,   ABT 1614 - 18 May 1683         Index

Individual Note:
     BIOGRAPHY: J.Gardener Bartlett, in his typed manuscript, gives teh following
record of Middlesex County Court, "On 3 April 1654, Thomas Hartshorn, aged
about 40, deposed in the Court of Middlesex Co., Mass. that Henry Felch
absented himself from church during baptism." So Thomas was born about 1614.
